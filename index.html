Avaliable Houses Types

Town House

Townhouses are rows of houses that share walls on either side with other neighbors. They come in various configurations, including those at the end of a row (which could be considered semi-detached) and those with outdoor yard spaces or patios.

Condo

Condos are apartment units typically found in multi-unit buildings or high-rises. In a condo, residents own what is inside their suite, while a condo corporation maintains the building common areas and exterior. Condos offer flexibility and are often located closer to downtown areas

Detached Condo

These stand-alone homes share no walls with other properties. They provide space, privacy, and often come with front and/or back yards, making them ideal for families. However, they tend to be more expensive than other housing types due to their size and exclusivity.
